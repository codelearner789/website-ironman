A Brief Introduction and Overview

I am Iron Man

- Tony Stark

Tony Stark aka Iron Man is a well-renowned superhero from the Marvel Cinematic Universe who uses his extremely advanced technological suits of armors to combat and defend the world. He is also part of the Avengers, a team of extraordinary individuals who uses their skills and strength to keep global threats at bay.

Traits of Tony Stark aka Iron Man

Personality

Tony Stark is someone who is often characterized as being arrogant and selfish. Due to his immense wealth and success, he often likes to see himself as the center of the universe. He is also quite stubborn and often likes to ignore the advice of others and usually just likes to stick with his own methods. However, after getting captured by terrorists in Afghanistan on a business trip and building a mechanized suit of armor to escape, he has slowly underwent a huge transformation in his personality. Although he still remains arrogant and is pretentious at times, he has shown a willingness to commit good deeds and sacrifice himself when needed. A famous example is when he risked his life to redirect the course of a nuclear missile fired at New York to save the city from annihilation and end the alien invasion in New York.

Abilities

As the Armored Superhero Iron Man

As Iron Man, Stark wears an electromechanical suit that gives him immense superhuman strength as well as an arsenal of weapons and equipment to aid him in his missions. His suit is also capable of flying at supersonic speeds as well as letting the user use the built in computer to analyze the suit's surroundings. Furthermore, the suit is essentially armed to the teeth with powerful repulsor blasters, missiles, and lasers.

Capabilities without his suit

Despite the strengths and immense capabilities, that the Iron Man suit offers, Tony Stark is much more than just the suit of armor he wears. He is gifted with an extremely brillant mind who graduated at the top of his class from the M.I.T at the tender age of 17. Some of his intellectual feats include building his first circuit board at age 4 and his own car engine at the age of six. His later feats include building a cold-fusion based arc reactor and his first iron man suit in a cave with limited resources as well as building an extremely sophisticated Artificial Intelligence system, Jarvis, from scratch.

On the Marvel Cinematic Universe

As a brief overview, the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) is defined as a film franchise with 20 films so far and fictional universe. The film that launched the franchise in 2008 was Iron Man. The franchise has grown to feature a diverse set of other superheros based on Marvel Comic Books since then. The total box office earnings for the franchise is collectively over 17.5 billion dollars.
